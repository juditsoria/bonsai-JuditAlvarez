Bonsai

The purposes of bonsai are primarily contemplation for the viewer, and the pleasant exercise of effort and ingenuity for the grower.

By contrast with other plant cultivation practises, bonsai is not intended for production of food or for medicine. Instead, bonsai practise is focused on long-term cultivation and shaping of one or more small trees growing in a container.
